About Us

At FalconX Hong Kong Limited, we are pioneers in rehabilitation technology. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals through innovative, effective, and user-friendly rehabilitation solutions. By integrating cutting-edge technology with a patient-centered approach, we strive to make recovery faster, safer, and more engaging.

Our Journey

  • 2015: FalconX Hong Kong Limited was founded with a vision to transform rehabilitation.
  • 2017: Launched our first modular rehabilitation robot.
  • 2020: Expanded our product line to include home-based rehabilitation solutions.
  • 2023: Introduced AI-driven rehabilitation technologies for enhanced patient outcomes.

Why FalconX Hong Kong Limited?

Choose FalconX Hong Kong Limited for our commitment to innovation, patient-centric design, and a proven track record in delivering effective rehabilitation solutions. Our expertise in robotics and rehabilitation technology sets us apart as a leader in the field, dedicated to improving the lives of patients worldwide.
